About    the   Designer

Sara Trajkoska, macedonian fashion designer with a passion for storytelling through design. I am constantly inspired by the world around me and strive to create designs that are elegant, clean and simple-looking.
Collection "Jagledasona"

In 2024, I launched my first collection, "Jagledasona". The collection is carefully designed with enormous care and passion, I aim to create fashion that will inspire people to feel comfortable and elegant, regardless of the occasion.
